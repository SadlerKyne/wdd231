NP Membership (Non-Profit)

Cost: No Fee

Benefits:

  • Basic listing in the Chamber directory.
  • Access to Chamber newsletters and communications.
  • Networking opportunities at select events.
×

Bronze Membership

Cost: $100/year (Example)

Benefits:

  • All NP Membership benefits.
  • Highlighted listing in the Chamber directory.
  • One complimentary admission to a Chamber workshop or seminar.
  • Discounted rates for Chamber events.
×

Silver Membership

Cost: $250/year (Example)

Benefits:

  • All Bronze Membership benefits.
  • Enhanced directory listing with logo.
  • Two complimentary admissions to Chamber workshops or seminars.
  • Opportunity to be featured in one newsletter per year.
  • Small banner ad placement on a subpage of the Chamber website.
×

Gold Membership

Cost: $500/year (Example)

Benefits:

  • All Silver Membership benefits.
  • Premium directory listing (top placement, larger logo).
  • Four complimentary admissions to Chamber workshops or seminars.
  • Priority consideration for event sponsorships.
  • Featured member spotlight on the homepage on a rotating basis.
  • Medium banner ad placement on the Chamber website.
  • Complimentary booth at one Chamber trade show or event per year.
