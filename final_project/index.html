How It Works

With our quick and easy process, we guarantee a smooth workflow from the time you open the PO to the time you receive your render or design.

Fill out the custom order form

You will fill out an order form with as much information as you can. If you have schematics and a BOM, upload them so our engineers have a detailed image of what you are wanting.

Book a consult

You are going to book a date and time for a one-on-one with our world-class engineers who will make sure they understand exactly what you are wanting. They will speak your language because we have engineers from most any field.

Receive your design

Depending on the depth of the project, you will receive your design in 7 to 20 days. This will be gone over in your initial sprint plan and again in any follow-ups.

In Stock Designs

These are designs that are not proprietary or that we have bought the rights to because we know it's something that can help the hard worker like you who needs parts fast.
